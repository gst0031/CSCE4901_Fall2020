About

Automatic Trend Detection: Time-Biased Document Clustering is a research paper by PhD candidate Sahar Behpour, Dr. Mark Albert, and team. Their paper delves into the topic of how time is often neglected when considering the features for text-mining algorithms, and how with their simple yet important addition, it can open a new world of analysis and even predict the rise and fall of research trends.

The goal of this site is to bring the results of this paper and their temporally-biased clustering approach into the mainstream by designing, coding, and implementing a dynamic yet easy to use web app that could change the direction of how future academic funds and interests are directed forever.
