Search Trending Topics

To be able to estimate in advance a trend topic in scientific research could be very beneficial. Funding agencies could properly allocate resources to research departments knowing that a certain topic is trending. The method proposed in this project analyzes trends emphasizing the role of time when identifying data clusters.

Dynamic Visualization

Click on individual clusters and see common keywords, authors, silhouette scores, standard deviation, etc. Understand the data being displayed to them through explanations on the website.
